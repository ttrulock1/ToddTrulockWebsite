Who We Are

La Frontera Center provides outpatient and residential mental health and substance abuse treatment services to children, youth, and adults in Pima County, Arizona.

This practice serves all patients regardless of ability to pay. No one will be denied access to services due to inability to pay. We have a discounted/sliding fee schedule available based on family size and income. Please enquire at the reception desk to apply.

Some of the Organizations underneath the LaFrontera website include Empact, providing outpatient behavioral health services, and The Tucson International Mariachi Conference that host mariachi and baile folklórico workshops.

West Point Apartments opens on Broadway

“West Point Apartments” located at 20 E Broadway Blvd., consists of 50 one-bedroom/one bath units that serve persons who are 55 and over. The project targets incomes between 40 and 60% of the area median income. The project is located in downtown Tucson near the Tucson Street Car. All utilities included. Supportive services are provided by La Frontera Center. Inc. Units based on availability.
